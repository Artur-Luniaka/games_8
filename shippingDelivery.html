6. International Shipping

Global Delivery

We ship to most countries worldwide. International orders are subject to:

  • Customs duties and taxes
  • Import restrictions
  • Additional processing time
  • Country-specific regulations

Customs & Duties

Import duties, taxes, and fees are the responsibility of the recipient. These charges are not included in our shipping costs and will be collected by the delivery service upon arrival.

Restricted Items

Some items may be restricted or prohibited in certain countries. We recommend checking your local customs regulations before placing an order.

7. Delivery Issues

Failed Deliveries

If delivery fails due to:

  • Incorrect address
  • Recipient not available
  • Access issues

The package will be returned to our facility. You may be charged a return shipping fee.

Damaged Packages

If your package arrives damaged:

  1. Do not accept the package
  2. Take photos of the damage
  3. Contact us immediately
  4. We'll arrange a replacement or refund

Lost Packages

If your package is lost in transit, we'll work with the shipping carrier to locate it. If it cannot be found, we'll provide a replacement or refund.
