1. Policy Overview

At GameVault Store, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. This policy outlines the conditions and procedures for returns and refunds for both physical and digital products.

Please read this policy carefully before making a purchase. By purchasing from our store, you agree to these terms.

2. Physical Products

Return Window

Physical products may be returned within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange, subject to the following conditions:

  • Product must be in original, unopened condition
  • Original packaging must be intact and undamaged
  • All included items must be present
  • Proof of purchase must be provided

Non-Returnable Items

The following items are not eligible for return:

  • Opened software or games
  • Personalized or custom items
  • Gift cards and digital codes
  • Items marked as "Final Sale"
  • Damaged or modified products

Return Shipping

Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the product is defective or we made an error. We recommend using a trackable shipping method and purchasing shipping insurance.

3. Digital Products

Refund Eligibility

Digital games and content are generally non-refundable once downloaded or activated. However, we may consider refunds in the following circumstances:

  • Technical issues preventing game launch or play
  • Billing errors or duplicate charges
  • Incorrect product purchased (within 2 hours of purchase)
  • Platform compatibility issues (if not clearly stated)

Refund Exceptions

Refunds will not be provided for:

  • Change of mind after activation
  • Performance issues on user's hardware
  • Dislike of game content or mechanics
  • Violation of platform terms of service

Digital Refund Process

To request a digital refund, contact our support team with your order number and detailed explanation of the issue. We will review your request and respond within 2 business days.

4. Defective or Damaged Items

Physical Products

If you receive a defective or damaged physical product:

  • Contact us within 7 days of delivery
  • Provide photos of the damage or defect
  • We will provide a prepaid return label
  • Replacement or refund will be processed upon receipt

Digital Products

For defective digital products:

  • Contact support with detailed error description
  • Include system specifications and error messages
  • We may request additional troubleshooting steps
  • Refund or replacement key will be provided if issue is confirmed

5. Return Process

Step 1: Contact Us

Email our customer service team at returns@gamevault.com with your order number and reason for return.

Step 2: Get Authorization

We will review your request and provide a Return Authorization Number (RAN) if approved.

Step 3: Package and Ship

Package the item securely with the RAN clearly visible on the outside. Ship to the address provided in your return authorization email.

Step 4: Refund Processing

Once we receive and inspect the returned item, we will process your refund within 5-10 business days.

6. Refund Methods

Original Payment Method

Refunds will be processed to the original payment method used for the purchase:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: 5-10 business days
  • PayPal: 3-5 business days
  • Digital Wallets: 1-3 business days

Store Credit

You may choose to receive store credit instead of a refund. Store credit:

  • Is issued immediately upon approval
  • Never expires
  • Can be used for any future purchase
  • Cannot be converted back to cash

Exchange Options

For eligible items, you may exchange for:

  • Same product in different size/variant
  • Different product of equal or lesser value
  • Store credit for the full purchase amount

7. Restocking Fees

In most cases, we do not charge restocking fees. However, the following fees may apply:

  • Opened Software: 15% restocking fee
  • Damaged Packaging: 10% restocking fee
  • Missing Components: 20% restocking fee
  • Return After 30 Days: 25% restocking fee

No restocking fees apply for defective items or returns due to our error.

8. International Returns

Shipping Costs

For international returns, customers are responsible for:

  • Return shipping costs
  • Customs duties and taxes
  • Import/export fees

Processing Time

International returns may take longer to process due to customs clearance. Allow 2-4 weeks for the complete return process.

Currency Conversion

Refunds will be issued in the original currency. Currency conversion fees may apply based on your bank's policies.
